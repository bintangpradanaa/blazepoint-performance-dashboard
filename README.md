## Project Overview
BlazePoint is a commercial company that requires performance monitoring system to evaluate its business operations across sales, profit, customer behavior, and fulfillment performance.

As a **BI Analyst**, I was responsible for designing and developing an interactive dashboard to help the **Head of Commercial** monitor business performance and support data-driven decision making. The dashboard consolidates key business metrics such as sales performance, profitability, order behavior, delivery performance, and customer segmentation.

## Goals
- Monitor overall business performance (Sales, Profit, Orders, AOV, Items Sold)
- Analyze trends of sales and profit over time
- Identify top contributing regions, segments, categories, and sub-categories
- Evaluate customer activity and order fulfillment performance
- Understand product performance and customer segmentation behavior

## Key Dashboard
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Items Sold
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Profit Trend Over Time
- Regional Performance
- Segment Contribution
- Category & Sub-category Performance
- Product Performance
- Delivery Performance (On-time rate, delay analysis)

## Output
- Business performance monitoring system with **Looker Studio Dashboard**
- KPI tracking for commercial decision-making
